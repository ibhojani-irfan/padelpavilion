Investment & CAPEX Strategy

The financial model utilizes the Mindspring Investment framework. Padel Australia injects capital via court assets, while Auma Holdings secures the physical location and operational readiness. Crucially, Auma's CAPEX now includes a $22,500 rental buffer to cover the 3-month shipping and installation window, ensuring financial stability pre-launch.

Operational Economics

"The Pav" operates on a Hybrid Staffing Model. Automated access during morning off-peak hours (6 AM - 2 PM) drastically reduces overheads, allowing the facility to remain profitable even at lower occupancy levels.
